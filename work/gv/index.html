Google for Veterans

Role: Visual Design

In order to better connect with their users, Google approached our team to help establish a system to reach out to specific demographics. In particular, they wanted to demonstrate how their services can help and support people, regardless of their situation and location. The first part of our engagement targeted the veteran community.

Team:

  • Associate Creative Director: Sam Lind
  • Interaction Designer: Rick Lee
  • Visual Designer: Alex Pytlarz, Linzi Bergmann, Michael Wang
  • Front-End Developer: Pete Schirmer, Lucas Shuman
